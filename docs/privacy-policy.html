🐾 Privacy Policy

Tap Trap Animal — Android Game

Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tap Trap Animal (“the Game”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application (package: com.spratap.taptrapanimal).

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The Game does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details such as your name, email address, or phone number.

All game data (scores, coins, unlocked animals, and preferences) is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences and is never sent to any server.

2. Third-Party Services

The Game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob is operated by Google LLC and may collect certain information to serve and personalise ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data collection is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from apps.

3. Permissions

The Game requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — Required by AdMob to load and display advertisements.
  • VIBRATE — Used for haptic feedback during gameplay.

4. Children’s Privacy

The Game is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through a third-party service embedded in the Game, please contact us and we will take steps to address the issue.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to retain on our side. Game progress stored on your device can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its storage in Android Settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
